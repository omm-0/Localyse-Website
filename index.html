Features

Everything You Need to Win Locally.

Smart Matching Engine

AI-powered matching that connects businesses with the most relevant local influencers based on audience, niche, location, and engagement quality.

Campaign Dashboard

Create, manage, and monitor all your campaigns from a single intuitive dashboard. No spreadsheets, no chaos.

Hyperlocal Discovery

Find influencers and businesses in your specific city, neighbourhood, or locality. No more irrelevant matches.

Verified Influencers Only

Every influencer on Localyse goes through audience quality checks. No fake followers, no inflated numbers — just real, verified engagement from real local people.

Direct Communication

Performance Analytics
